Tennis Western Grip

The Western Grip is one of the three main racket grips used in tennis. It is the toughest of the three to learn, and because of that most people tend not to use it. But for those few that do use it, they will have some of the scarriest forehands in their games. Because the Western grip offers unparalleled topspin.

Tennis Eastern Grip

The Eastern Grip is one of the three main racket grips used in tennis. It is considered the easiest of the three and is tought to beginners. But don't be fooled by how easy it is to learn. It offers the most power of the three grips.
